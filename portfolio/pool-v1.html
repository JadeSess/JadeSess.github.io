Problem
Tickets to high in demand events are sold out within seconds to scalpers thus placing high economic barriers on attendees and keeping fans out of seats.

Solution
A centralized ticket pool that regulates the sale and transfer of irreproducible tickets. The pool is where users can purchase tickets, return tickets, or watch sold out events.
